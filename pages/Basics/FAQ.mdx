# KeepKey FAQ

## How To

1. **How do I recover my device from seed?**
Check out our [recovery guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **How do I send Bitcoin with my KeepKey?**
Refer to our [Sending Bitcoin guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

3. **How do I update my firmware?**
Follow our [Updating your KeepKey guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

4. **How do I use the Pin cipher?**
See our guide on [Understanding the KeepKey PIN](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

5. **How do I wipe my KeepKey?**
Check our [How to wipe your KeepKey guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

6. **Can I use a KeepKey with my Phone?**
No, KeepKey requires a USB connection to KeepKey Desktop and is only compatible with Windows/MacOS/Linux.

7. **What Coins can I use with KeepKey?**
View our [coin support page](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

8. **I can't find XRP/DASH/DGB in my dashboard. How do I use my Ripple?**
ShapeShift has removed support for these assets. You can find our new [standalone applications](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery) for managing these assets.

## General

1. **Is KeepKey Open Source?**
Yes! KeepKey firmware has a deterministic build process. The community can audit every release we have made over the last 9 years. More information: [Deterministic Build process](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **How do I buy crypto?**
ShapeShift makes it simple to buy/sell crypto and is integrated natively inside KeepKey Desktop! Read more about [ShapeShift here](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

3. **Can I trade and swap crypto?**
Yes! Our native Thorchain integration allows frictionless native swapping without using wrapped tokens on bridges. Read more [here](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

4. **Is KeepKey owned by ShapeShift DAO?**
No, KeepKey is owned and operated by former employees of ShapeShift independently. KeepKey continues to fund the ShapeShift DAO. [KeepKey Workstream](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

5. **How do I use ShapeShift with my KeepKey?**
ShapeShift is listed in our dapp store. [How to open ShapeShift](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

## Troubleshooting

1. **My KeepKey is not recognized by my computer**
Check out our [troubleshooting guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **I can't get ShapeShift.com or KeepKey Desktop to recognize my device, what do I do?**
You can use KKcli to update your firmware. [Using KKcli to debug your KeepKey](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

3. **What do I do if I lose my PIN?**
Wipe your device and proceed with recovery.

4. **What do I do if I lose my KeepKey?**
Purchase a new KeepKey and proceed with recovery.

5. **What do I do if I lose my Recovery Phrase?**
Purchase a new KeepKey. Initialize and set up the new device. Transfer your funds to the new device.

## General Security

1. **What makes KeepKey unique compared to other hardware wallets?**
Read about the industry and the reasons behind the prevalence of myths when it comes to hardware wallet security. [5 Myths about Hardware Wallets](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **Can I self-custody bitcoin between multiple devices with multi-sig?**
Yes, check out our [Electrum Multi-sig Guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

3. **How do I protect my funds from physical attacks, for instance a stolen or lost device?**
Protect your assets from physical attacks with a BIP39 Passphrase. [Securing your KeepKey](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

## Web3 Community

Yes, you can use Uniswap and most web3 applications via wallet-connect. See our [dApp guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **How do I participate in DAO governance with my KeepKey?**
You can use the [Snapshot dapp guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

## Developer Community

1. **How do I Integrate KeepKey into my app or service?**
See our developer guide for [building multi-chain dApps with the KeepKey-sdk](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **What is the KeepKey REST API?**
KeepKey REST API is the primary interface for integrating KeepKey into your application. [KeepKey REST API](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

3. **A dapp I use is not listed in the dapp store, how do I get it listed?**
You can submit the dapp yourself! Chart the dapp for others to follow. [Charting a DAPP on Pioneer platform](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

4. **How do I support KeepKey on my Web3 based application?**
If you are a Web3 project, we recommend onboard.js and enabling wallet-connect. [onboard.js developer documentation](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).
